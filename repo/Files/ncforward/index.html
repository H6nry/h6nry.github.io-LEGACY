Should be compatible with iOS 5.0 - 8.x

NCForward lets you forward your iOS notifications to ANY other device. Currently, Windows is supported, Mac and Linux are not.
You will need to run this program on your Windows computer to forward your notifications within your LAN.
To forward your notifications to Pushbullet devices, sign in to Pushbullet in settings.
For further information and documentation read here: NCForward - Forward your notifications
