Should be compatible with iOS 5.0 - 8.x

NCForward lets you forward your iOS notifications to ANY other device in your local network (LAN/WLAN). Currently, Windows is supported, Mac and Linux are not.
You will need to run this program on your Windows computer to make this tweak usable. For further information and documentation read here: NCForward - Forward your notifications
